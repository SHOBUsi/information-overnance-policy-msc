# LD7006 – Information Governance and Security

## Overview
A comprehensive information governance policy developed for *Cafe de Fantastica*, 
a fictional UK café chain. The policy covers data lifecycle management, regulatory 
compliance, risk management, and incident response.

## Key Areas Covered
- UK GDPR & Data Protection Act 2018 compliance
- ISO/IEC 27001, ISO 22301, ISO 31000 alignment
- PCI-DSS requirements for payment data
- Data classification, retention, and disposal
- Employee training and awareness frameworks
- Business continuity and disaster recovery

## Skills Demonstrated
- Policy writing and governance framework design
- UK data protection law application
- Risk assessment methodology (NIST SP 800-30)
- Information security management standards
